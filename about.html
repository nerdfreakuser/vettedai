How VettedAI Works

We built an autonomous AI engine that researches products, writes honest reviews, and keeps everything updated — 24/7.

STEP 1

AI Research

Our AI agent scans the web for trending products, reads expert reviews, compares specs, and aggregates user sentiment from forums and social media.

STEP 2

Content Generation

Using the research data, the AI writes comprehensive, SEO-optimized review articles with real pros/cons, comparison tables, and honest ratings.

STEP 3

Affiliate Integration

Each review includes curated buy-links from trusted retailers. When you purchase through our links, we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

STEP 4

Distribution

New reviews are automatically published to the site and promoted across social channels to drive organic traffic.

Revenue Model

Affiliate Commissions

Primary revenue from Amazon Associates, direct brand partnerships, and SaaS referral programs. Typical commission: 3-15% per sale.

Newsletter Sponsorships

As the subscriber base grows, weekly newsletter placements become a secondary revenue stream. Rates scale with audience size.
