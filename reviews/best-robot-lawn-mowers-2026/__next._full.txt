<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-4"><li class="list-disc">  <strong>Wired vs. Wireless:</strong> Wired mowers are cheaper and work perfectly under heavy tree cover, but installation is tedious. Wireless (RTK/GPS/Vision) mowers are easy to set up and mow in straight lines, but they are expensive and need clear views of the sky.</li>
<li class="list-disc">  <strong>Yard Size and Slopes:</strong> Check the manufacturer's maximum acreage rating. If you have steep hills, look for all-wheel-drive models like the Mammotion LUBA.</li>

</ul>
