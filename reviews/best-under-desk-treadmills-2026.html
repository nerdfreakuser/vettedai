Why You Need an Under-Desk Treadmill in 2026

The line between the home office and the home gym has completely blurred. With more professionals working remotely than ever, staying active during a nine-to-five shift is a top priority. Under-desk treadmills (also known as walking pads) are the perfect solution, allowing you to hit your 10,000 steps without stepping away from your laptop.

Unlike traditional, bulky treadmills, the latest models for 2026 are incredibly sleek, whisper-quiet, and designed to easily slide under your standing desk or sofa. In this comprehensive review, we dive into the best walking pads of 2026, comparing them on motor noise, belt size, weight capacity, and overall value.

If you spend hours on Zoom calls, drafting emails, or coding, adding a walking pad to your setup is the ultimate productivity hack.

1. Goplus 2-in-1 Folding Treadmill (Our Top Pick)

The most versatile walking pad for both light walking and light jogging.

The Goplus 2-in-1 Folding Treadmill takes our top spot for 2026 because it seamlessly bridges the gap between a standard walking pad and a traditional workout treadmill. When the handrail is folded down, it operates as a quiet under-desk walking pad (up to 2.5 MPH). When the handrail is raised, it transforms into a jogging treadmill capable of speeds up to 7.5 MPH.

Powered by a 2.25 HP motor, the Goplus is surprisingly quiet, ensuring you won't disrupt your colleagues on a conference call. It features a shock-absorbing running belt that protects your knees and joints, and a built-in Bluetooth speaker so you can listen to your favorite podcasts while you walk.

This model features a wider walking area than typical budget models, making it much more comfortable for taller users or those with a wider stride. The noise reduction technology is top-tier, featuring an 8-point shock absorption system that dampens the sound of every footfall. The smart connectivity syncs effortlessly with your Apple Watch or Fitbit to log your indoor walking miles accurately.

3. Merach Walking Pad Under Desk Treadmill

The best mid-range option for daily home office use.

If you want a reliable, no-frills walking pad that simply works, the Merach Walking Pad is a fantastic choice. Priced very reasonably, it offers a sturdy build, a straightforward LED display tracking your time, speed, and distance, and a lightweight frame (only 68 lbs) that makes it easy to wheel around.

The Merach utilizes a durable alloy steel frame and a quiet motor that hums along unobtrusively. It also comes with a longer 41.3-inch belt, which is a rare find in this price bracket, accommodating taller workers who need a slightly longer stride while focusing on their screens.

The best budget-friendly walking pad.

Getting fit while working shouldn't require emptying your wallet. The AKLUER Under Desk Treadmill proves that you can get a highly functional walking pad for under $150. It’s incredibly compact, making it the perfect choice for small apartments or tight cubicles.
