<h2>2. Amazon Kindle Scribe: Best for Readers Who Like to Scribble</h2>

<p><strong>Price:</strong> $339</p>
<p><strong>Pros:</strong>

<h2>3. Onyx Boox Note Air 3 C: The Jack-of-All-Trades Android Tablet</h2>

<strong>Who it's for:</strong> Power users who need their tablet to integrate with their existing app ecosystem (Evernote, Notion, etc.) and want the versatility of color.</p>
<h2>4. Supernote A5 X2: The Organization Powerhouse</h2>
<p>Supernote has carved out a loyal following by focusing relentlessly on workflow and organization. The A5 X2 doesn't have the premium metal build of the reMarkable or the app store of the Boox, but it features the smartest software for keeping track of your thoughts.</p>

<ul class="list-disc pl-6 mb-4"><li class="list-disc">The hardware looks and feels a bit plasticky compared to its sleek rivals.</li>

<li class="list-disc">Smaller ecosystem of third-party accessories.</li>
