Upgrading your front door with a smart lock is one of the most impactful smart home upgrades you can make in 2026. Gone are the days of fumbling for keys in the dark or leaving a spare under the doormat. Today’s best smart locks offer keyless entry via fingerprints, smartphone apps, keypads, and even smartwatches, all while keeping your home highly secure.

But with so many options on the market, choosing the right one can be overwhelming. Do you need a lock that works with Apple HomeKit, Google Assistant, or Amazon Alexa? Are you looking for a full deadbolt replacement or something that retrofits over your existing hardware?

We’ve reviewed the top smart locks of the year based on security, ease of installation, smart home compatibility, and overall value. Here are our top picks for 2026.

What to Look for When Buying a Smart Lock

When shopping for a smart lock in 2026, keep these crucial factors in mind:

  1. Compatibility: Make sure the lock works with your existing smart home platform (Alexa, Google, Apple Home). If you want the lock to communicate with other devices (like turning on the hallway lights when you unlock the door), platform compatibility is essential.
  2. Lock Type: Decide whether you want a full deadbolt replacement (which looks cleaner and often includes a keypad) or a retrofit lock (which is easier to install and perfect for renters).
  3. Connectivity: Wi-Fi locks connect directly to your router for easy remote access, but they drain batteries faster. Bluetooth locks have better battery life but require a plug-in Wi-Fi bridge or a compatible smart home hub (like an Apple TV or Amazon Echo) for remote control.
  4. Access Methods: Consider how you want to open the door. Keypads are great for guests and kids without smartphones. Fingerprint readers offer the fastest entry. Apple Home Keys are incredibly convenient for iPhone users.
