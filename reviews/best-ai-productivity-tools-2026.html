The AI productivity landscape has exploded. Every week brings a new tool promising to 10x your output. But which ones actually deliver? We spent 30 days testing the most popular options across writing, project management, note-taking, and automation.

Our Top Picks

1. Notion AI — Best All-in-One Workspace

Notion has integrated AI deeply into every surface. From auto-generating project docs to summarizing meeting notes, it's become the Swiss Army knife of productivity.

Pros:

2. Raycast Pro — Best for Developers

Raycast's AI extensions turn your launcher into a powerhouse. Quick translations, code explanations, and clipboard history with AI search make it indispensable.

Pros:

3. Granola — Best AI Meeting Notes

Granola sits quietly in your meetings and produces structured, actionable notes. It understands context and extracts action items automatically.

Cons:
