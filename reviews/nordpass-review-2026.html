What is NordPass?

NordPass is a password manager developed by Nord Security — the same company behind NordVPN, one of the world's most trusted VPN providers. Launched in 2019, NordPass has rapidly matured into a full-featured password vault with a clean interface and a security-first architecture.

Its core job: store your passwords, credit card numbers, secure notes, and personal info in an encrypted vault — and autofill them automatically across every device you own. It does this job very well.

Where NordPass stands apart from rivals like 1Password and Bitwarden is its choice of encryption algorithm: XChaCha20, which is faster and considered more future-proof than the AES-256 standard used by most competitors. For security-conscious users, this matters.

Security & encryption

XChaCha20 encryption

NordPass uses XChaCha20 — a modern stream cipher favoured by Google and Cloudflare for its speed and security. It's mathematically equivalent to AES-256 in terms of protection but faster on devices without hardware AES acceleration (most mobile devices).

Zero-knowledge architecture

NordPass can never read your passwords. Your vault is encrypted and decrypted only on your device using your master password. Nord Security's servers only ever see encrypted blobs — they have no technical ability to access your data.

Independent audits

NordPass has been independently audited by Cure53 — a respected German cybersecurity firm. The audits found no critical vulnerabilities. This level of transparency puts NordPass ahead of many competitors who have never had a public audit.

Multi-factor authentication

NordPass supports TOTP-based 2FA (Google Authenticator, Authy etc), hardware security keys (FIDO2/U2F), and biometric login on mobile and supported laptops. This is the baseline you'd expect — and it delivers.

Features & usability

NordPass covers every major feature you'd expect from a modern password manager, plus a few extras that are genuinely useful.

Password vault

Store unlimited passwords, credit cards, secure notes, and personal info. The vault UI is clean and fast to navigate.

Autofill

Browser extensions for Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, and Opera. Autofill is smooth and accurate — handles login forms, sign-up pages, and payment fields.

Passkey support

NordPass supports passkeys — the passwordless future of authentication. Store and use passkeys across your devices just like regular passwords.

Data breach scanner

NordPass continuously monitors whether your email addresses or passwords have appeared in known data breaches. Premium feature — alerts you immediately if something is compromised.

Password health checker

Identifies weak, reused, or old passwords across your vault. Gives you a clear list to action, making cleanup quick and painless.

Emergency access

Grant a trusted person access to your vault in an emergency. They request access, you get a window to deny it — after which it grants automatically.

Cross-device sync

NordPass is available on Windows, macOS, Linux, iOS, Android, and all major browsers. Sync is seamless and fast. The free tier only allows one active device at a time — Premium unlocks unlimited simultaneous devices, which is the main reason to upgrade.

Import from other managers

Moving to NordPass from LastPass, 1Password, Bitwarden, or others is straightforward. It supports CSV import from all major password managers — takes about 5 minutes to migrate a full vault.

NordPass vs 1Password
1Password has slightly more power-user features (Travel Mode, Watchtower). NordPass wins on price — especially the 2-year plan. For most people, NordPass is the better value.
NordPass vs Bitwarden
Bitwarden is cheaper and open-source, which some users prefer. NordPass has a cleaner interface and better breach scanning. Bitwarden is better for self-hosters; NordPass is better for most people who want a polished experience.
NordPass vs LastPass
After LastPass's catastrophic 2022 breach, we struggle to recommend it. NordPass wins on security architecture, transparency (third-party audit), and breach track record. This is an easy call.
