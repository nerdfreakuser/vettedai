In 2026, the global streaming landscape is more vibrant and fragmented than ever before. With content increasingly siloed by regional licensing agreements, a reliable Virtual Private Network (VPN) has transitioned from a niche tool to an essential utility for any serious streamer. Whether you're trying to catch up on a show available only in another country, bypass bandwidth throttling from your ISP, or simply secure your connection while enjoying your favorite series, the right VPN is your key to unlocking a truly borderless entertainment experience.

To help you navigate this crowded market, VettedAI rigorously tested the leading VPN services against criteria critical for streaming in 2026. Our evaluation focused on unblocking capability across major platforms (Netflix, Disney+, Hulu, BBC iPlayer, etc.), consistent high speeds for 4K and HD content, server network size and global distribution, ease of use across various devices, and robust security features to protect your data without compromising performance. We put these services through their paces to ensure they deliver on their promises of seamless, secure, and unrestricted streaming.

1. ExpressVPN – Best Overall

Price: ~$99.99

ExpressVPN continues its reign as the top choice for streaming in 2026, delivering unmatched speed and reliability. Its Lightway protocol ensures buffer-free viewing across numerous platforms, making geo-restrictions a thing of the past for discerning streamers. While it comes at a premium, its consistent performance justifies the investment for a truly global entertainment experience.

Pros:

  • Blazing-fast Lightway protocol
  • Consistent unblocking of major platforms
  • Vast global server network
  • Intuitive apps on all devices
Cons:
  • Higher annual subscription cost
  • Fewer simultaneous connections than some competitors

2. NordVPN – Best for Speed & Security

Price: ~$89.99

NordVPN excels with its proprietary NordLynx protocol, offering some of the fastest speeds crucial for 4K streaming. Beyond just speed, its integrated SmartPlay feature simplifies access to geo-blocked content, while strong encryption provides peace of mind. It’s a powerful all-rounder for those prioritizing both performance and digital security in their streaming setup.

Pros:

  • NordLynx protocol for exceptional speeds
  • Robust security features (Threat Protection)
  • SmartPlay DNS for easy streaming access
  • Extensive global server count
Cons:
  • Desktop app can occasionally be complex
  • Inconsistent unblocking with very niche regional services

3. Surfshark – Best Budget Pick

Price: ~$59.99

For streamers on a budget who need to cover multiple devices, Surfshark is an unbeatable option. Its unlimited connections policy and remarkably low price make it incredibly cost-effective, while still delivering solid unblocking capabilities and speeds suitable for most HD content. It’s the smart choice for families or households with many streaming devices looking for great value.

Pros:

  • Unlimited simultaneous device connections
  • Excellent value for money
  • Reliable unblocking of popular services
  • Decent speeds for HD streaming
Cons:
  • Speeds can be slightly less consistent than premium rivals
  • Fewer advanced configurations for power users

4. ProtonVPN – Best for Privacy-Focused Streamers

Price: ~$119.99

ProtonVPN stands out for users who prioritize privacy alongside their streaming habits. While it might not always boast the absolute fastest speeds, its robust security infrastructure, including Secure Core, ensures your viewing habits remain confidential. For accessing geo-restricted content with an added layer of anonymity and trust, ProtonVPN offers a compelling, albeit premium, solution.

Pros:

  • Strong privacy and audited no-logs policy
  • Secure Core servers for enhanced security
  • Decent speeds on paid Plus plans
  • Good unblocking for major platforms
Cons:
  • Higher price point for premium features
  • Speeds can vary more on certain servers
  • User interface less intuitive for beginners

The Verdict: Which Should You Buy?

After extensive testing, it's clear that the best VPNs for streaming in 2026 excel in speed, unblocking, and user experience. ExpressVPN remains our top recommendation for its unparalleled consistency and lightning-fast speeds, making it the go-to for serious streamers. NordVPN impresses with its blend of speed and security, offering a robust option for those who want both performance and protection. For budget-conscious users or large households, Surfshark delivers exceptional value with unlimited connections, while ProtonVPN stands out as the premier choice for privacy-focused individuals who won't compromise on security while they stream.
